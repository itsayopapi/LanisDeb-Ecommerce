Product Description

The Royal Gold Aso Oke Set represents the pinnacle of Nigerian traditional fashion. Crafted from premium Aso Oke fabric, this ensemble showcases the rich cultural heritage of the Yoruba people while incorporating contemporary design elements for modern elegance.

Each piece in this set is meticulously handcrafted by skilled artisans who have mastered the traditional techniques of Aso Oke weaving and embroidery. The intricate gold threadwork against the deep purple background creates a striking visual contrast that commands attention and admiration.

This complete set includes:

  • Elegant Buba (top) with elaborate gold embroidery
  • Flowing Iro (wrapper) with complementary patterns
  • Matching Gele (headpiece) for a complete look
  • Optional Ipele (shoulder piece) available separately

Ideal for weddings, naming ceremonies, cultural festivals, and other special occasions where you want to make a statement of elegance and cultural pride.
